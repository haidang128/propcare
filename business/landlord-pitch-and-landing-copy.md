# PropCare — Pitch & Landing Copy

## Positioning (internal)

For London landlords with 1–10 properties who currently fix things by phoning "their guy", PropCare is a repairs service with **vetted tradespeople, a fixed price shown before you book, and a timestamped maintenance record for every property**. Unlike hourly services (Aspect et al.), the price can't drift; unlike software tools (FixRoute, Landlord Studio), we actually send the tradesperson.

## Elevator pitch (30 seconds)

"I'm a landlord too, and getting repairs done was always the same mess — chasing trades, no idea what it'd cost, nothing written down. So I built PropCare: you send a photo of the problem, see a fixed price up front, approve it, and a vetted local tradesperson handles it. You watch the status live instead of phoning anyone, and every job lands in a permanent record per property — which matters now the Renters' Rights Act means you need to prove how fast you respond to repairs."

### How it works

**1. Snap & send** — Photo, description, and how urgent it is. Takes two minutes.
**2. Fixed price up front** — Our rate card price appears before you commit. If the job turns out bigger on site, work pauses until you approve a new fixed price in the app. You are never billed more than you approved.
**3. We handle access** — Your tenant picks a time slot from a simple link. No app, no account, no you-in-the-middle.
**4. Watch it happen** — Live status, before/after photos, and payment by secure link only after the work is done.

### Why landlords switch

**A price before, not a bill after.** Hourly trades bill you for time. We publish fixed prices per job — a dripping tap costs the same whether it takes 20 minutes or 90.

**Vetted, insured, certified.** Every tradesperson is identity-checked and carries public liability insurance. Electrical work only ever goes to NICEIC/NAPIT-registered electricians — the app physically won't assign it to anyone else.

**Your repairs, on the record.** Every request, photo, price, and completion is timestamped per property. With the Renters' Rights Act now in force — and Awaab's Law repair timescales coming to the private sector — "I dealt with it promptly" needs evidence. Export any property's history as a PDF in one tap.

**Emergencies, honestly priced.** Out-of-hours jobs run at a published surcharge, best-effort. If no one's available we tell you immediately and show you exactly what to do in the meantime — including how to shut off the water.

### Trust / FAQ

**Who does the work?** Local, vetted, self-employed tradespeople we work with directly — not a marketplace of strangers. We check ID, insurance, and certifications before their first job and track expiry dates continuously.

**What if the job is bigger than described?** Work pauses. You get a new fixed price in the app with photos of why. Nothing proceeds until you tap approve.

**When do I pay?** After completion, by secure payment link (card, Apple/Google Pay, bank). If you raise a dispute the payment is held until it's resolved.

**What about my tenant's details?** We use their contact only to arrange access, tell them who we are, and delete it 90 days after the job. Full privacy policy → [link]
